Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for the event?

add
You can register directly through the "Register Now" buttons on this website. Registration is handled via our secure IEEE portal. Early bird discounts are available for members.

Is the registration fee refundable?

add
Partial refunds are available until January 20th. After this date, registrations are non-refundable but can be transferred to another participant from the same organization.

Is accommodation included in the fee?

add
No, the registration fee covers all technical sessions, delegate kits, lunches, and high tea for both days. Accommodation must be booked separately; please refer to our suggested hotels for discounts.
